#### Homemade Switches Assignment!
In this assignment, I was tasked to do two homemade switches so that when both were pressed at the same time the LED would light up. One tip that I would give to my future self would be to be more mindful of which side I was working on. I was talking with my roommate when I realized I had sewn the LED on the same side as the battery and therefore had to restart.
