#### Help, I Need Somebody Assignment!
In this assignment, I was tasked to code a program that play out two different morse code patterns depending on whether or not the circuit was open or closed.  One tip for my past self would be to take a closer look at the suggested tips in the slides as I tried to define my letters as integers before realizing that I should have made it a void instead.
