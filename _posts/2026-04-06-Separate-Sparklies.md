#### Separate Sparklies (Mid-Semester Project)!
In this assignment, I was tasked to code three different Arduino programs representing the three different fireflies and different behaviors of each of them. One tip for my past self would be to not split my Arduino board as that made recording the process more difficult as I couldn't figure out how to connect the broken off piece using the alligator clips. So I couldn't take photos of the pins lit up and had to record a video for all three of them instead.
