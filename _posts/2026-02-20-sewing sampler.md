#### Sewing Sampler Assignment!
![Front side of sewing sampler](https://jennyzhao.github.io/assets/img/front-sampler.jpg)
This is the front side of my sewing sampler! I did a running stich, back stich, satin stich, stem stich, cross stich, blanket stich, whip stich, a chain stich, and a split stich. 

![Back side of sewing sampler](https://jennyzhao.github.io/assets/img/back-sampler.jpg)
This is the back side of my sewing sampler. One tip that I would include for past me doing this assignment is to be careful with the tension for the satin stich as it is very prone to bunching.
