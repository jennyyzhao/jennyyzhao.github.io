#### Ghost Detector!
In this assignment, I was tasked to code a program that would detect the temperature and the light to turn on the red LEDs on my board to scare away the ghosts. One tip for my past self would be to include the sensor pin (A2) as I thought that the seperate attachment (A9) could do both temperature and light and got confused when it didn't.
